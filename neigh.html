About The Project

This mobile application allows users to see what events are happening in their Neighborhood and helps users to engage with their communitiy. The app also allows users to save upcoming events or report on the news that is happening in their area.



The Neighborli mobile app is designed and laid out so that the interface delivers a lot of info in an uncluttered space. The market research led to the conclusion that the app needed to feel like a newspaper without feeling overwhelming and the layout had to be structured similarly to that of an online social media platform like facebook.

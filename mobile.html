About The Project

This mobile application allows users to see snippets of news article about Bravo reality shows. The app starts with a guided tour of how to set up daily reminders to check the news and moves on to showcase the articles in a clean and concise manner.

The application is engaging and straighforward. The user personas made during initial testing indicated that people were looking for quick and easily scannable news sources when it came to seeing what was up with their reality show stars. The personas also concluded that there needed to be set times that people would be reminded to check the app so that they would not have to worry about forgetting to check up on Bravo news.
